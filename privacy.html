Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 15, 2026

Application

This Privacy Policy applies to Oleh Auto Poster, a personal automation tool used by the owner to prepare and publish their own image posts to Pinterest, Instagram, and TikTok.

Data processed by the app

The app may process images, captions, publishing settings, board identifiers, and API access tokens provided by the owner.

How data is used

The data is used only to prepare and publish social media posts requested by the owner. The app does not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Third-party services

When publishing content, the app may send the provided image URL, caption, and publishing metadata to connected platforms such as Pinterest, Instagram, and TikTok.

Data storage

The app stores configuration and post history locally on the owner's computer. Public image files may be hosted on this GitHub Pages site for publishing purposes.
