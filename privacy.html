Oleh Auto Poster

Oleh Auto Poster is a personal publishing assistant used by the owner to prepare and publish their own image posts to Pinterest, Instagram, and TikTok.

The app is used for personal content management and does not provide a public user-facing service.

Features

  • Prepare image posts with captions
  • Publish owned content to Pinterest boards
  • Prepare publishing payloads for connected social platforms
